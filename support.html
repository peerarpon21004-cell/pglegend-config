Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress on a new device?

Progress is currently stored locally on each device. Cloud sync (iCloud) is planned for a future update.

The app shows a "Maintenance" screen — what now?

The game is briefly offline for an update. Most maintenance windows are under 30 minutes. Try again shortly.

How do I reset my progress?

Open the game → tap the gear icon (top-left of Home) → Save Data → Reset All Progress. Action is irreversible.

Are there in-app purchases?

Not currently. All in-game currency is earned through play. Future cosmetic-only IAP may be added.

I found a bug — where do I report it?

Email peerarpon21004@icloud.com with:

  • iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 15)
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshot if possible
